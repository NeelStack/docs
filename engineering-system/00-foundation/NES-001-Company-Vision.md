# NeelStack Company Vision

> **"To build products that millions trust and engineers admire."**

---

# Executive Summary

NeelStack exists to build technology that solves meaningful problems while establishing one of the world's most respected engineering organizations.

Our ambition extends beyond creating successful software products.

We aspire to create an engineering company recognized for exceptional architecture, disciplined engineering, long-term thinking, and relentless execution.

Every decision made within NeelStack should strengthen this vision.

Every product should contribute to a larger ecosystem.

Every engineer should contribute to a stronger engineering culture.

This document defines the long-term vision that guides every business decision, engineering investment, product strategy, hiring decision, and technology choice.

# Decision Filter

Every important decision should pass the following questions.

1. Does this align with our vision?
2. Will this strengthen the platform?
3. Does this improve engineering quality?
4. Will this help future engineers?
5. Will customers benefit?
6. Can this scale?
7. Is it maintainable?
8. Would we make the same decision five years from now?

If the answer is "No" to several questions, reconsider the decision.
